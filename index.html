About Me

I'm Goldan Mahardhika, a passionate 3D Artist specializing in Modeling and Lighting.
I got several experiences as Render Artist at Mocca Studio and 4 Crows Digital. I do teamworking on 3D Lighting set, Rendering and Compositing with Blender and Nuke Software.

One of the biggest project i ever work is “Lil Wild Season 3” an Children’s television Animation series. I'm currently exploring the intersection of 3D art and the Web3 ecosystem.
