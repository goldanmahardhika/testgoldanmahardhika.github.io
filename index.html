About Me

I'm Goldan Mahardhika, passionate in 3D Lighting and Rendering. I have experience as a Render Artist at Mocca Studio and 4 Crows Digital (Animation Studios based in Malang).

I specialize in teamwork for 3D Lighting sets, Rendering, and Compositing using Blender and Nuke. One of the biggest projects I've worked on is “Lil Wild Season 3”, a children’s television animation series.
